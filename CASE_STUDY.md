# Project Case Study

## Project Name

MANA Yerba Mate – Interactive 3D eCommerce Website

## Business Overview

MANA is a premium beverage brand offering organic Yerba Mate drinks.

The company required a unique digital experience that would differentiate the brand from traditional beverage websites while maintaining a fully functional online shopping experience.

---

## Challenge

Traditional eCommerce websites often focus only on product listings and transactions.

The goal of this project was to create a highly engaging and visually immersive experience capable of:

- Increasing user engagement
- Strengthening brand identity
- Improving product discovery
- Supporting direct online sales
- Delivering a memorable user experience

---

## Solution

A modern interactive website was developed featuring:

- 3D product interactions
- Animated storytelling
- Dynamic page transitions
- Shopify eCommerce integration
- Mobile responsiveness
- Optimized performance

The result is an immersive shopping experience that combines branding, education, and purchasing within a single platform.
